We are The Chicken or The Egg

But we raise more than just chickens and we produce more than just chicken eggs.

Our farm is home to a diverse community of birds, including chickens, ducks, geese, and quails, all raised with love and respect. We prioritize ethical farming methods, ensuring that our birds roam freely in open pastures, indulging in natural behaviors and forging connections within their flocks.

As advocates for transparency, we invite you to explore our farm, meet our feathered residents, and witness firsthand the ethical practices that set us apart. Join us in supporting a sustainable and humane approach to farming, where the well-being of our birds is paramount, and the result is a bounty of delicious, ethically sourced eggs.

At The Chicken or The Egg, we're not just cultivating eggs; we're cultivating a community that values ethical choices, sustainability, and the joy of connecting with nature. Thank you for being a part of our journey towards a more compassionate and conscientious future.
